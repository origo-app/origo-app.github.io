Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 08, 2025

Introduction

Origo is an app designed to enhance your digital experience by offering personalized content based on your preferences and activity. This Privacy Policy describes the information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your personal data.

Who We Are

Origo is operated by Rotcol LLC, a company based in New York, USA, at 143 Ludlow Street, New York, NY 10002, USA. You can contact us at help@origo-app.com.

Information We Collect

  • Information You Provide Directly: When you sign up for the app or interact with it, we collect information such as your name, email address, date of birth and any content you upload or save within the app. This information is provided directly by you.
  • Device and Technical Information: When you use the Origo app, we collect information about the device you use to access our services, including the type of device, operating system, and device identifiers. We also collect information through log data, which may include your IP address, browser type and activity within the app.
  • Usage Data: We gather data on how you use the app, such as which features you interact with, what content you engage with, and how frequently you use the app. This helps us understand your preferences to improve your experience.
  • Third-Party Information: We do not currently collect any information from third-party platforms or advertisers.

How We Use the Information

  • Provide and Improve the App: We use your information to deliver and enhance your experience within the app. This includes offering personalized content based on your preferences and activity.
  • Communicate with You: We may use your information to respond to your inquiries, provide customer support, and notify you of important updates regarding the app.
  • Safety and Legal Compliance: We may use your information to detect and prevent fraud, enforce our terms of service, and comply with legal obligations.

Cookie and Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies to collect or store personal information. All data used for personalizing content is processed in real-time and is not stored persistently through cookies.

How We Share Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties for advertising purposes. However, we may share information in the following circumstances:

  1. With Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party vendors who assist us in operating the app and providing services on our behalf. These service providers are required to handle your information securely and only for the purposes specified by us.
  2. For Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or if we believe that such action is necessary to comply with legal processes, protect our rights, or ensure the safety of others.
  3. Corporate Transactions: If Rotcol LLC undergoes a merger, acquisition, or other corporate transaction, we may transfer your information to the acquiring party.

Your Choices and Rights

You have certain rights regarding your information. Depending on your location, these may include:

  • Access and Correction: You can request access to the information we hold about you and make corrections if needed.
  • Data Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and personal data by contacting us at help@origo-app.com.

Privacy Rights under the GDPR

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you, including the purposes for which it is being used. Requests can be made via email to help@origo-app.com and we will respond within a reasonable time.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete data we hold about you.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request the deletion of your personal data when it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected or if you withdraw your consent.
  • Right to Object: You can object to the processing of your personal data for marketing purposes or for other legitimate interests.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request that we provide your personal data in a structured, commonly used format for transfer to another service provider.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: If you believe we have infringed on your privacy rights, you can lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Privacy Rights for California Residents under the CCPA

If you are a resident of California, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants you the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Know: You have the right to request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal data we have collected about you, the sources of the data, and the purposes for which it is used.
  • Right to Delete: You can request that we delete your personal data, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out: While we currently do not sell your personal data, you have the right to opt-out of the sale of personal data should we choose to do so in the future.
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your rights under the CCPA.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at help@origo-app.com.

Children’s Privacy

The Origo app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. If you are a resident in a jurisdiction with a higher age requirement, you can only use the app if you meet the applicable age requirement.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at help@origo-app.com and we will take steps to remove such information.

How Long We Retain Your Information

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services to you and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. When we no longer need your data for these purposes, we will either delete it or anonymize it so that it can no longer be used to identify you.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you by email or through the app. By continuing to use the app after such changes, you agree to the updated policy.

Contact Us

For any questions or to exercise your rights under this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: help@origo-app.com.
